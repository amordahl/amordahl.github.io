About Me

I am a third year Ph.D. student at The University of Texas at Dallas. My faculty supervisor is Dr. Shiyi Wei.

Research Interests

I am primarily interested in making mobile and desktop software safer using static analysis tools to detect bugs and privacy leaks. My research thus far has focused on better understanding the capabilities of configurable static analysis tools and diagnosing sources of imprecision and unsoundness.
